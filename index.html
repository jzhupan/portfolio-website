Hi, I'm Jennie

After a few years of freelancing in illustration and graphic design, I took a bold leap into programming, choosing to specialize in front-end development. Initially, I thought this transition would be smooth, but it turned out to be a significant challenge, transforming into a thrilling journey of growth and learning. This shift required me to leave behind the familiar and embrace a determined path forward. As part of this commitment, I've adopted a practice of diligently making daily commits on GitHub. This routine isn't just about pushing big chunks of code for projects but also about maintaining a consistent engagement with my development work, reflecting my dedication to continuous improvement. Alongside this, I'm constantly striving to enhance my front-end development designs. Integrating my background in illustration and graphic design, I aim to create visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces that stand out. Every step in this journey not only sharpens my technical abilities but also reinforces my passion and dedication to evolving as a developer.
